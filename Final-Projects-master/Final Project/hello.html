Help Someone:

Want to help, but not sure what to do?

It may appear to be little, however simply doing things together, being there and remaining associated can be a major help. If you are the main support person for someone going through depression or Suicide it can be rewarding because your helping people with this sistuation and givng them support if there alone.
  • pick a place that’s quiet and private
  • listen, more than talk. Really listen closely to understand how they’re feeling
  • save your advice for later
  • show you’re really listening
  • sit face-to-face so they can see your reactions
